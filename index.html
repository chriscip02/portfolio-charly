TITULO A DEFINIR

Charly Tanoni has been doing sound and music design since he was a teenager, venturing into different groups that played on the stages of the Buenos Aires underground (Buenos Aires, Argentina) ,being a musician and producer; taking the role of both. (Ritual Cielo, DDT, Blitto, Bulevard Tango Club, among others)

In 2004 he began to make music, sound design and audio posts for different platforms, series, films, video games, brands, agencies and personalities from the artistic world who need to express themselves or deepen their work. From his production company (TANONI&CO.) Carlos says: “…we create many types of work, from feature film soundtracks and sound design, custom museum projects to audio concepts for major products and brands.” He creates experiences that push the boundaries of listening and engage new audiences with audio through completely free but universally organized sounds.

We produce creative original music and sound design for commercial, motion design, broadcast, video games and VR experiences.
